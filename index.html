The Metropolitan Museum of Art, "Met Museum" located in New York City, was created on April 13th, 1870, which is 149 years go. The museum is the largest art museum in the United States with around 7 million visitors to it three locations in 2018. The museum contains a permanent collection of over two million different art works which is divded among seventeen departments.
